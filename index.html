INTERNATION SCHOOL (JIRS)

Hi!

Jain International Residential School (JIRS), Bangalore is an institution also with weekly boarding facilities under The JGI Group on a 350 acres (1.4 km2) campus known as the Jain Global Campus which is JGI's main campus. JIRS has more than 750 students from India and from other countries. Jain Academy For Sporting Excellence (JASE) also operates on the campus with professional coaching since 2004. Classes for SAT, JEE are also conducted. It is a certified ISO 9001:2008 for international quality being an IB World School.
